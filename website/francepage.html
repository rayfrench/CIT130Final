Paris is without a doubt one of the most beautiful cities in the world, the capital of France, of art, of fashion. There are a million things to do and see in Paris whether you visit alone or with a loved one. No matter how long you plan to learn French there, it'll never be long enough: Climb to the top of the Eiffel Tower, walk down the Avenue Champs Elysees, visit the Louvre Museum, go to Euro Disney, enjoy the parks, gardens, boutiques ...

Why you should visit France

  • Fine wine
  • French cuisine
  • Paris and the Eiffel Tower
  • Winter sports haven
  • Fashion
  • Art, culture and cinema
